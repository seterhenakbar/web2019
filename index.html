Our R&D Processes

These processes are designed to be independent, modular and efficient so you can choose the right combination for your R&D project challenge. Each of the processes have pre-defined workflow, input & output possibilities. You can mix and match them to suit your needs.

Co-Creation Workshop

Co-Creation is our bread and butter service. It is a very versatile tool to empathize, ideate and define a problem statement with multi stakeholders point of view.


Outputs:

  • Problem Definition
  • Customer Persona
  • Customer Journey
  • Low-Fidelity Prototype
  • Product Backlog

In-Depth Interview

Understanding your users needs is the most important part of product development. Great products can only come from deep insights. What better way to understand your customer other than converse with them directly.

Outputs:

  • Customer Insights

User Testing

We all have assumptions. And we tend to bring a lot of our assumptions to our design. User testing will help you validate your design assumptions to ensure that you have put yourself in your customer's shoes.

Outputs:

  • Design Validation
  • Design Audit

Design Sprint

Software developments are complex and tend to be resources draining. It is very important to ensure we have a validated design ready before we hit the pedal in developing actual software. Design sprint will minimize the risk of developing the wrong thing early in the pipeline.

Outputs:

  • UI & UX Design
  • Medium Fidelity Mockup
  • Interactive Mockup

Agile Development

When we finally have the experience design ready for development, it is time to actually build the product. Even then the risk of uncertainties are still high enough. You will want to develop the software in a responsive way in adapting with customer's feedback.

Outputs:

  • Mobile Application
  • Web Application
  • Interactive Installation
  • Firmware Application
